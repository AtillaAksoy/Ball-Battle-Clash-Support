# Ball Battle Clash

**Ball Battle Clash** is a fast arcade arena game where colorful battle balls collide, grab weapons, collect hearts, and fight to be the last one standing.

Jump into quick mobile battles, control your own ball with smooth joystick controls, fire at the right moment, dodge enemies, and survive the arena chaos. Each round is short, energetic, and different.

## Features

- Fast arcade ball battle gameplay
- Mobile joystick controls
- Manual fire challenge mode
- AI enemy battles
- Spectator mode
- Weapons, hearts, and arena action
- Physics-based collisions
- Quick portrait mobile matches
- Regular updates with new content, improvements, modes, weapons, and arenas planned over time

## Game Modes

- **Control Mode:** Move your own ball and survive the arena.
- **Challenge Mode:** Control movement and manually fire your weapon.
- **Hard Challenge:** Enemy balls aggressively hunt weapons.
- **Expert Mode:** A tougher setup with stronger enemies and limited tools.
- **Spectator Mode:** Watch the chaos unfold automatically.
