# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Ball Battle Clash is a mobile arcade game published by Atilla Aksoy.

## Information We Collect

Ball Battle Clash does not require an account and does not ask players to enter personal information inside the game.

The game may use third-party advertising services, including Google AdMob. These services may collect or process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, diagnostics, app interactions, and ad interaction data according to their own policies.

## Children

Ball Battle Clash is an arcade game with cartoon/fantasy combat and weapon visuals. The game does not include chat, user-generated content, gambling, loot boxes, or unrestricted web browsing.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Advertising partners may process data for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, analytics, and service improvement according to their policies.
