# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Ball Battle Clash is a mobile arcade game published by Atilla Aksoy.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Ball Battle Clash.

## Information We Collect

Ball Battle Clash does not require an account and does not ask players to provide personal information such as name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, camera access, or microphone access.

The game may store gameplay preferences locally on your device, such as player name and control layout settings. These settings are used only to improve your gameplay experience and remain on your device unless removed by the app or your device settings.

## Advertising and Third-Party Services

Ball Battle Clash uses Google AdMob to show advertisements.

Google AdMob and related Google services may collect or process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, diagnostics, app interactions, and ad interaction data. This data may be used for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, analytics, and service improvement according to Google's policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Advertising Technologies:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information directly with other users.

Third-party advertising providers, including Google AdMob, may process data as described in their own privacy policies.

## Children

Ball Battle Clash is a cartoon arcade game with fantasy combat, weapon visuals, and advertisements. The game does not include chat, user-generated content, gambling, loot boxes, or unrestricted web browsing.

The game is not specifically directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable law.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to keep the app experience safe. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated over time as Ball Battle Clash receives new features, platform changes, or service updates. Any changes will be posted on this page.
